About Us

Welcome to ANU — where flavor meets soul.

At our heart, we’re more than just a restaurant — we’re a place where culinary traditions come alive and every dish tells a story. Whether it’s the smoky aroma of our tandoor specials, the delicate richness of our biryanis, or the playful creativity in our mocktails and desserts, every plate is a celebration of taste. Founded with a passion for good food and warm hospitality, our goal has always been simple — serve memorable meals that bring people together. We combine age-old recipes with modern flair, using only the freshest ingredients and authentic spices to bring you a dining experience that feels both familiar and new.
